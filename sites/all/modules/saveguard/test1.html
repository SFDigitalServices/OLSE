Thank you for updating your account information with the Office of the Treasurer and Tax Collector. You will receive an email confirmation for your records, and your "Reminder to File" notifications will now be adjusted based on the information you submitted.

 

If you do not receive the confirmation email within 24 hours (hint: check your "spam" or "junk" folder), and then submit a request for assistance.

 

If you have questions or need to update the information provided, you may submit your request electronically and a customer service representative will contact you. Please note that taxpayers who come to City Hall for in-person assistance or call 311 may experience long wait/hold times.

 

A list of frequently asked questions is available under the “What if” section.
